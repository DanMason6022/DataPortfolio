About Me:

Hi, I'm Dan!

I graduated from Durham University in the summer of 2024 with a degree in Natural Sciences, which just means a combination of 2 or more subjects from the BSc selection. I picked Physics and Computer Science. At first, I wasn’t sure what my next steps would be. I hoped I'd find some inspiration during my time at Uni. Maybe in the form of pursuing further education (like a masters or maybe even a PhD)? But it turned out that wasn't for me. I enjoyed my time at Uni, but the BSc was quite enough! It wasn't until the summer after my graduation, that I stumbled upon the idea of working in data. Since then, I've been dedicated to expanding my knowledge and skills in this dynamic field.

This site is a work in progress, aiming to showcase my journey and growth as I prepare to confidently step into the competitive job market. I’m excited to share my projects, insights, and experiences with you as I continue to learn and evolve. Thank you for visiting!
