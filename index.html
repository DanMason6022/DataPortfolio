About Me:

Hi, I'm Dan.

I graduated from Durham University in the summer of 2024 with a degree in Natural Sciences — a flexible program that lets you combine multiple BSc subjects. I chose Physics and Computer Science.

Like many graduates, I wasn’t entirely sure what direction I wanted to take after university. I considered staying in academia — maybe a master’s or PhD — but ultimately decided I was ready to move on and explore new challenges.

After graduating, I found a real interest in working with data. Since then, I’ve been building up my skills and diving into projects that combine analytical thinking with technical problem-solving.

This site is a work in progress, created to document my journey and showcase what I’m learning along the way. I’ll be sharing my projects, insights, and experiences as I learn.

Thanks for visiting!
